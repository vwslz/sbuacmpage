Winners of the CompProg ICPC Selection Contest 2022

Thanks to all participants of the CompProg ICPC Selection Contest 2022 and congratulations for the great performance!

The contestants were given 8 algorithmic programming problems to solve in 3 hours. Each contestant had to participate alone.

Here are the Winners of the contest in each category. Greg Zborovsky became the overall Champion by correctly solving 6 out of the 8 problems!
